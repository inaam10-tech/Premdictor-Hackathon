About Us Page

Welcome to our web application! Using complex prediction algorithms and real-time Premier League statistics, we have developed a mathematical model that will predict the performance of teams in the Premier League

The Model uses real-time player SPI ratings to help predicted a teams strength. Then it simulates all the games in 100 premier league seasons, using weighted randomizers for team performance, injuries, home field advantage and many other factors. After simulation the average points achieved by the team and average; wins, losses, and draws is outputted on to the website as seen.
